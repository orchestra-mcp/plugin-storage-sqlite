# Team tunnel sharing

Allow tunnel owners to share their tunnels with team members. Invite by email or team role. Shared tunnels appear in the team member's tunnel list with a 'shared' badge. Owner can revoke access anytime. Team-wide tunnels: a team admin can register a tunnel that's accessible to all team members. Workspace isolation: each team member sees only their own workspace context on shared tunnels.
